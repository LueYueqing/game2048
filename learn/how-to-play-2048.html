üéØ Basic Strategies for Beginners

üìç Corner Strategy

Keep your highest tile in one corner (preferably bottom-right). This prevents it from getting in the way and gives you more space to work with smaller tiles.

üìê Edge Priority

Always try to keep larger numbers on the edges of the board. This makes it easier to merge them and prevents chaos in the middle of the board.

üîÑ Consistent Direction

Choose one main direction (down or right) and stick to it for most of your moves. This helps maintain order and makes planning easier.

üö´ Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not Planning Ahead: Always think 1-2 moves ahead
